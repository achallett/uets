What is Unix Time?

Unix Time (also frequently known as Epoch Time) is a method for measuring time. Unix Time is the number of seconds that have occurred since January 1st 1970 at 00:00:00 UTC (excluding leap seconds). As Unix Time relies on UTC, Unix Time does not change anywhere around the globe. Coordinated Universal Time (abbreviated UTC) is the time by which the world regulates time. UTC is the successor of GMT (Greenwich Mean Time), which is the mean solar time at the Royal Observatory in Greenwich, London, England.
